====== 39.In uyan si Hisus diye te Hukmanan ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-01.jpg}}

Te liware e te marusilem, in uyan e si Hisus te me sundalu diye te labew ne talagpanubad eyew eg-insaan. Ne si Pidru migsinsinundul kandan piru diye de te bayew madmariyu. Ne nataman inpaseled e si Hisus diye te baley, ne si Pidru nahaat de diye te lihawangan ka migginarang te hapuy.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-02.jpg}}

Te diyad e te seled te baley, Inbusuran e insei si Hisus te labew te me Hudiyu. Ne dutu masalig ka migpamalehet te ubat diye ki Hisus piru ware nenekeg-iling ka me inpanlalag dan, sikannaa ka kene egkapamalehetan ne due sale ni Hisus. Ne si Hisus migpepeeneng de ne ware lalahan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-03.jpg}}

Ne migmemetmetan te labew ne talagpanubad si Hisus ne migkahiyan, "Guhuri key, sikeykew iya ka Misiyas, ne Anak te ne uyag ne Manama?"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-04.jpg}}

Ne migtabak si Hisus, "Si Keddi iya, ne egkakita niyu kangkuwa ne egpinnuu a ne duma te Manama wey eglibed a puun te langit." Ne nabelu ka labew ne talagpanubad wey ingisi din e ka kumbale din, ne migkahi diye te me pangulu, "Kenad keilangan ne duen pad tigpamalehet! Nakarineg kew mismu te inlalag din ne sikandin kun Anak te Manama. Ne key ka iggukum niyu?"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-05.jpg}}

Kalangun ne pangulu te me Hudiyu migtabak diye te labew ne talagpanubad, "Eleg si kandin ne eggimatayan



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-06.jpg}}

Taheed te egtetahad si Pidru diye te lihawangan te baley, due suluhe-anen ne malitan ne nakakita kandin ne migkahi, "Talag duma kew ki Hisus!" Ne mig-uubat si Pidru. Ne duen e ma e lein ne malitan ne migkahi si Pidru duma ni Hisus. Piru mig-uubat ded man e si Pidru. Te natagtaheed e migkahi e ka me etew, Nakanengneng key ne duma ka iya ni Hisus su matig Galiliya kema.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-07.jpg}}

Piru migkahi si Pidru, 'Eg tungluwen a te Manama ke nekeila a te seini ne etew!" Sahuhune ne mig-ukale e ka manuk. Ne miglingey e si Hisus ka migpitew diye ki Pidru.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-08.jpg}}

Ne miggipanew e si Piru wey migsinehew e migpanamantaman. Te pegkanengnengi ni Hudas te igukuman e si Hisus te kamatayen, nalaggew e amana si kandin ne nataman migluhee din e.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-09.jpg}}

Early the next morning, the Jewish leaders brought Jesus to Pilate, the Roman governor. They hoped that Pilate would condemn Jesus as guilty and sentence him to be killed. Pilate asked Jesus, “Are you the king of the Jews?”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-10.jpg}}

Jesus answered, “You have said so, but my kingdom is not an earthly kingdom. If it were, my servants would fight for me. I have come to earth to tell the truth about God. Everyone who loves the truth listens to me.” Pilate said, “What is truth?”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-11.jpg}}

After speaking with Jesus, Pilate went out to the crowd and said, “I find no guilt in this man.” But the Jewish leaders and the crowd shouted, “Crucify him!” Pilate replied, “He is not guilty.” But they shouted even louder. Then Pilate said a third time, “He is not guilty!”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-39-12.jpg}}

Pilate became afraid that the crowd would begin to riot, so he agreed to have his soldiers crucify Jesus. The Roman soldiers whipped Jesus and put a royal robe and a crown made of thorns on him. Then they mocked him by saying, “Look, the King of the Jews!”



// A Bible story from: Matthew 26:57-27:26; Mark 14:53-15:15; Luke 22:54-23:25; John 18:12-19:16 //
