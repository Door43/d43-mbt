Nataman, miggingume e ka panalihan ne mig-uyan te lalag te Manama diye te buyag e ne talagpanubad ne migngaranan ki Sakariyas. Amana ne matinumanen te Manama si Sakariyas wey ka asawa rin ne si Ilisabit, piru ware de due nakaanak si Ilisabit.

Ne migtabak ka panalihan, "Insuhu a te Manama eyew igpanengneng keykew ka meupiya ne panugtulen. Piru tenged su kene ke ma egpalintutuu keddi, kene kad egpakalalag taman te eglesut e ka bate." Ne sahuhune ne kenad egpakalalag si Sakariyas. Ne in-engkeran e sikandin te panalihan. Pegkapenga due, mig-uli e si Sakariyas wey egkaberes en iya ka asawa rin.

When Elizabeth was six months pregnant, the same angel suddenly appeared to Elizabeth’s relative, whose name was Mary. She was a virgin and was engaged to be married to a man named Joseph. The angel said, “You will become pregnant and give birth to a son. You are to name him Jesus. He will be the Son of the Most High God and will rule forever.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-05.jpg}}

Ne migtabak si Mariya, "Menuwen naa sika te ware e mem pad neyilabeti?" Ne impasabut e sikandin te panalihan, "Eggendiniyan ka te Matulus ne Panisingan te Manama, wey egdalungan ka te geem din. Purisu egmatulus ka bate, ka Anak te Manama." Ne migpalintutuu si Mariya wey indawat din e ka inlalag te panalihan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-22-06.jpg}}

Te ware de naluhey puun te migpakiglalag ka panalihan ki Mariya, migpanumbaley e sikandin diye ki Ilisabit. Ne nakarineg de iya si Ilisabit ki Mariya te migpahinlewuran, migweil e ka bate diye te getek din. Ne dutu naneggale e ka daruwan ne malitan te inggimu te Manama kandan. Te migtatelu e ne bulan si Mariya diye ki Ilisabit, mig-uli e sikandin.
