====== Ka peglansang ki Hisus ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-01.jpg}}

Pegkapenga te me sundalu ne migdali-di ki Hisus, in-uyan dan e sikandin eyew iglansang te krus. Impatiang dan kandin ka krus ne eglansangan kandin wey diye, egpatey e sikandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-02.jpg}}

Ne in-uyan e te me sundalu si Hisus diye te inged migngaranan te "ka Kalabira" wey inlansangan dan e ka me belad wey paa ni Hisus diye te krus. Piru migkahi si Hisus, "Ame, pasayluwa nu sikandan su ware dan nakataha te inggimu." Ne migpasulatan ni Pilatu te "Hari te me Hudiyu" ka dibadew te krus dapit te ulu ni Hisus.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-03.jpg}}

Ne immegpusteey e ka me sundalu te kumbale ni Hisus. Ne tenged due, natuman e ka pegpanagne ni migkahi, "Imbaad-baad dan ka kasangkapan ku, wey immegpusteey e sikandan te me kumbale ku."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-04.jpg}}

Inlansang si Hisus diye te taliware te daruwa ne takawen. Ne indali-dian si Hisus te sabeka ne takawen, piru migkahi ka sabeka, "Ware ke bes naaldek te Manama? Makasesale ki piru ware sale te seini ne etew." Ne migkahiyan din e si Hisus, "Kena a lingawi nikeykew diye te trunu nu." Ne migtabak e si Hisus ka migkahi, "Kuntee ne aldew, egpakaruma kad keddi diye te Paraisu!"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-05.jpg}}

Ne indali-dian si Hisus te me pangulu te me Hudiyu wey te duma pad ne me etew diye te kalibulungan. Ne igkahiyan dan sikandin, "Emun ke anak ka te Manama, pamaneug ka te krus wey luwasa nu ka pegkeetew nu! Ne egpalintutuu key e keykew."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-06.jpg}}

Ne migmarusilem e amana ka langit te intiru ne inged minsan meudtu pad seeye. Ne migtatelu ne uras ka migmarusilem.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-07.jpg}}

Ne migpanguleyi e si Hisus ka migkahi, "Ame, nataman e! Igbehey kud keykiew ka Panisingan ku." Ne in-useng e sikandin wey imbehey rin e ka Panisingan din. Te impatey e sikandin, miglilinug e, wey nahisi e ka maluag ne kurtina diye te eletanan te me etew wey te Manama diye te timplu--puun de iya te dibabew peendiye te diralem.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-08.jpg}}

Through his death, Jesus opened a way for people to come to God. When the soldier guarding Jesus saw everything that had happened, he said, “Certainly, this man was innocent. He was the Son of God.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-09.jpg}}

Then Joseph and Nicodemus, two Jewish leaders who believed Jesus was the Messiah, asked Pilate for Jesus’ body. They wrapped his body in cloth and placed it in a tomb cut out of rock. Then they rolled a large stone in front of the tomb to block the opening.



// A Bible story from: Matthew 27:27-61; Mark 15:16-47; Luke 23:26-56; John 19:17-42 //
