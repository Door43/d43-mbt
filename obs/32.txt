====== 32. Migbawian ni Hisus ka migserepan te busew,wey ka Malitan ne migderalu ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-01.jpg}}

Sabeka ne aldew, mig-untud e si Hisus wey ka me hibateen din te balangey wey miggendiye dan e te inged te in-upaan te me matig-Girasa.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-02.jpg}}

Te miggingume e sikandan diye te talipag te lanew, duen e etew ne migserepan te busew ne migpallahuy peendiye ki Hisus.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-03.jpg}}

Amana ne nenekali seini ne etew wey warad iya egpakaleheng kandin. Layun de egbebakua sikandin te me etew te me keddina, piru egmambugtusen din de iya seini layun.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-04.jpg}}

Ne diyad in-ugpe ka etew, marani te sabalanan. Ne iyan din egpulunganan se egpangpanguleyi te intiru ne aldew wey marusilem. Warad iya kukumbaleen wey egpamalpalian din te me batu ka lawa rin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-05.jpg}}

Te migparani e sikandin diye te tangkaan ni Hisus, migpanimbuel e sikandin. Ne migkahiyan e ni Hisus ka busew, "Busew, lihawang ka puun te seini ne etew!"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-06.jpg}}

Ne migpataman-taman e migkuleyi ka etew ne inserepan te busew ne migkahi, "Nekey ka eggimuwen nu keddiey, Hisus ne Anak te Amana ne Mabantug ne Manama? Kena a ubag legpari nikeykew!" Ne in-insaan e sikandin Hisus, "Hentew ka ngaran nu?" Ne migtabak ka busew ne migkahi, "Si Matangen ka ngaran ko, uya su musulug ney ma." (Ka "matangen," punduk te libu-libu ne me sundalu te Ruma.)



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-07.jpg}}

Ne migpeyid-uhid-u e ki Hisus ka me busew ne migkahi, "Kene key re ubag paawaa te seini ne inged!" Ne diye te marani ne bubungan, due masalig ne me babuy ne migpanuwal. Purisu migpeyid-uhid-u e ka me busew ki Hisus ne migkahi, "Seeye key re ubag ipaserep te me babuy!" Ne migkahi e si Hisus, "Hendiye kew-e!"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-08.jpg}}

Ne dutu, miglihawang e ka me busew puun te etew, wey migserep e diye te me babuy. Ne namallahuy e ka me babuy ne nanupang diye te dalama wey neneulug e diye te lanew wey nenallened e. Me daruwa ne libu ne gatus ka kasuluhan te me babuy.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-09.jpg}}

Te nakite e te me talagtameng te me babuy ka neyibu, nangendiyad sikandan te lungsud ka migpangguhud ke nekey ka nakita ran ne inggimu ni Hisus. Ne nangingume e ka me etew puun te lungsud wey nakita ran e ka etew ne inserepan te me busew ne migpinnu e, duen e kumbale, wey maheteng e ka suman-ssuman din.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-10.jpg}}

Ne nenaaldek e amana ka me etew, sikan naa ka impaawe dan e si Hisus. Purisu in-untud e si Hisus te balangey eyew eg-awe e. Ne miggangyu e ka etew ne inserepan te busew ne egduma kandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-11.jpg}}

Piru migkahiyan sikandin ni Hisus, "Kene egkaayun. Uli kad e diye te baley niyu wey pangguhuri nu ka me alukuy nu, pamilya, te langun ne inggimu te Manama keykew, wey te keyid-u rin keykew."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-12.jpg}}

Purisu miggipanew e ka etew wey impangguhuran din e ka langun meyitenged te inggimu ni Hisus diye te kandin. Ne amana ne nenatanengan ka langun ne nakarineg te guhuren te etew.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-13.jpg}}

Jesus returned to the other side of the lake. After he arrived there, a large crowd gathered around him and pressed in on him. In the crowd was a woman who had suffered from a bleeding problem for twelve years. She had paid all of her money to doctors so they would heal her, but she only got worse.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-14.jpg}}

She had heard that Jesus had healed many sick people and thought, “I’m sure that if I can just touch Jesus’ clothes, then I will be healed, too!” So she came up behind Jesus and touched his clothes. As soon as she touched them, the bleeding stopped!



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-15.jpg}}

Immediately, Jesus realized that power had gone out from him. So he turned around and asked, “Who touched me?” The disciples replied, “There are many people crowding around you and bumping into you. Why did you ask, ‘Who touched me?’”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-32-16.jpg}}

The woman fell on her knees before Jesus, shaking and very afraid. Then she told him what she had done, and that she had been healed. Jesus said to her, “Your faith has healed you. Go in peace.”



// A Bible story from: Matthew 8:28-34; 9:20-22; Mark 5:1-20; 5:24b-34; Luke 8:26-39; 8:42b-48 //
