====== Ka guhuren meyitenged te... ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-01.jpg}}

Sabeka ne aldew, in-insaan e ni Pidru si Hisus te migkahi, "Ahalen, kapapila ku egpasayluwa ka me suled ku ke egpakasale sikandan keddiey? Taman te igkapitu?" Ne migtabak si Hisus ka migkahi, "Kene ne kapapitu re, ke kene, kapapituwan ne pulu!" Ka igpasabut ni Hisus due ne keilangan iya ne egpasaylu ki layun. Ne impangguhud en iya ni Hisus ka seini ne guhuren.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-02.jpg}}

Ne migkahi si Hisus, "Iling te Hari ne egpanukut te utang te me suluhuanen din ka migharian te Manama. Ne due sabeka ne suluhuanen din ne nekeutang te bali te daruwa ne gatus ne libu (200,000) ne leg-un ne laslas te buel."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-03.jpg}}

"Ne tenged su kene egpakabayad ka suluhuanen te utang din, migkahi e ka Hari, 'Ibelegye nu seini ne etew wey ka pamilya rin eyew egpakabayad sikandin te utang din.' "



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-04.jpg}}

"Ne impanimbuel e ka suluhuanen diye te tangkaan te Hari ka migkahi, "Ahalen, behayi e pa ubag nikeykew te timpu eyew egpakabayad a te utang ku keykew.' Ne neyimenawan e kandin ka Hari, purisu inlingawan e te Hari ka langun ne utang din wey inlekaan e sikandin te Hari."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-05.jpg}}

"Piru te nakalihawang ka suluhuanen puun te Hari, nakita rin e ka duma rin ne suluhuanen ne nekeutang kandin te bali te hep-at ne bulan ne laslas te buel. Ne inggen-genan din e ka duma rin suluhuanen te migkahi, 'Bayari nu ka utang nu keddiey!' "



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-06.jpg}}

"Ne impanimbuel e ka duma rin ne suluhuanen ka migkahi, 'Behayi e pa ubag nikeykew te timpu eyew egkabayaran ku ka utang ku keykew.' Piru, nasi din de iya ipapirisu ka duma rin taman te nakabayad e sikandin te utang din."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-07.jpg}}

"Ne due duma ne me suluhuanen ne nakakita te neyitabu, wey amana ran nabantali. Ne miggendiye dan e te Hari wey inlalahan dan e ka Hari te neyitabu."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-08.jpg}}

"Ne impeumew e te Hari ka suluhuanen wey migkahiyan din sikandin, 'Mareet ka himu nu! Inlingawan kud ka utang nu tenged su miggangyu ka. Iling ded perem degma ka inggimu nu due!' Nalanglangetan amana ka Hari wey impapirisu rin e ka suluhuanen taman te nakabayad e sikandin te langun ne utang din."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-29-09.jpg}}

Then Jesus said, “This is what my heavenly Father will do to every one of you if you do not forgive your brother from your heart.”
