====== F>PARUSA ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-01.jpg}}

Ne miggendiye si Muwisis wey si Aarun te ki Paraun wey ingkahiyan dan sikandin, "Migkahi ka Manama te Israil 'Ipalihawang nud ka keet-etawan ku!' " Piru ware migpamineg si Paraun kandan. Nasi din de iya pehesa sikandan te egpatrabahu patamtamani.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-02.jpg}}

Ne eg-apul de iya layun si Paraun te ingkahi te Manama ne egpalihawangen ka me etew. Purisu migpeuyan e ka Manama te sapulu ne makaliyas-liyas ne karereetan diye te Ihiptu. Ne pinaahi te sika ne karereetan, impakita te Manama ki Paraun ne maresen pad iya sikandin ki Paraun, wey te langun ne diyus-diyus te Ihiptu.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-03.jpg}}

Inggimu te Manama te langesa ka Weyig te Nilu, piru kene ded iya man-e igpalihawang ni Paraun ka me matig-Israil.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-04.jpg}}

Nataman, impeuyan e man-e ka Manama te me bakbak diye te intiru Ihiptu. Ne inggangyu e ni Paraun si Muwisis ne eg-aween ka me bakbak. Piru te nenapatey e ka me bakbak, makehal ded iya man-e ka ulu ni Paraun, wey kene din iya igpalihawang puun te Ihiptu ka me matig-Israil.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-05.jpg}}

Purisu impeuyan e man-e ka Manama te susuluhi ne me langew. Ne impeumew e man-e ni Paraun wey si Muwisis, wey inlalahan din e sikandan te egpakalihawang e ka me matig-Israil ke egpaaween dan e ka me langew. Te in-ampu e si Muwisis, in-awe e te Manama ka langun ne langew puun te Ihiptu. Piru makehal ded iya man-e ka ulu ni Paraun wey kene din ded iya igpalihawang ka me etew.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-06.jpg}}

Nataman, impareralu e te Manama ka langun ne ayam te me matig-Ihiptu wey nataman ne impamatey e seeye. Piru amana iya ne makehal ka ulu ni Paraun, wey kene din iya igpalihawang ka me matig-Israil.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-07.jpg}}

Ne ingkahiyan e te Manama si Muwisis te igpasawed kandin ka alinepung diye te tangkaan ni Paraun. Ingkasawed din de iya seeye ne duen e egpanubu ne sesakiti ne me kehang diye te me lawa te me matig-Ihiptu, piru ware mule tubui kayi ka me matig-Israil. Impakehal te Manama ka ulu ni Paraun, wey kene din iya igpalihawang ka me matig-Israil.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-08.jpg}}

Ne pegkapenga due, impeuyan e man-e ka Manama te apiyew ne nakarereet te me pinamula te me matig-Ihiptu wey nekeyimatey te minsan hentew ne diye te lihawangan. Ne impeumew e ni Paraun si Muwisis wey si Aarun wey ingkahiyan din sikandan, "Nakasala a. Kaayun kew e ne eg-awe." Purisu mig-ampu e si Muwisis, wey mig-engked e ka apiyew.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-09.jpg}}

But Pharaoh sinned again and hardened his heart. He would not let the Israelites go free.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-10.jpg}}

So God caused swarms of locusts to come over Egypt. These locusts ate all the crops that the hail had not destroyed.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-11.jpg}}

Then God sent darkness that lasted for three days. It was so dark that the Egyptians could not leave their houses. But there was light where the Israelites lived.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-10-12.jpg}}
