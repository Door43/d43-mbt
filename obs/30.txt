====== F>INPAKEEN ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-01.jpg}}

Ne insuhu e ni Hisus ka me apustulis din eyew egwali wey egpanulu te me etew diye te lein-lein ne me inged. Te nakalibed e sikandan diye te in-ugpaan ni Hisus, inlalahan dan e sikandin te inggimu ran. Ne indume e sikandan ni Hisus diye te deyipag te lanew diye te kene ne mahulub eyew egdagdahew eggimeley. Ne in-untud sikandan te balangey wey neendiye dan e te deyipag te lanew.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg}}

Piru masulug ka me etew ne nakakita ki Hisus wey te me hibateen din ne mig-untud te balangey. Ne mig-ilid e te lanew ka sika ne me etew ne namallahuy peendiye te talipag te lanew eyew egpekewun-a engki Hisus. Purisu te pegginguma enni Hisus duma te me hibateen din, diyad e ka susuluhi ne me etew ne egtetahad kandan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-03.jpg}}

Subla te lalimma ne libu ka me etew ne nalibulung diye ware labet te me malitan wey me bate. Ne neyimenawan e amana si Hisus te me etew su iling sikandan te me karniru ne ware talagtameng. Purisu impanulu e ni Hisus ka me etew, wey imbawian din e ka duma ne egmanderalu.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg}}

Ne nataman, te maapun e, migkahiyan e si Hisus te me hibateen din, "Talagpanulu, maapun e wey ware me inged kayi te marani. Ipeuli nud ka me etew eyew egpakakuwe e sikandan te egkakeen dan."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg}}

Piru ingkahiyan ni Hisus ka me hibateen din, "Behayi niyu sikandan te egkakeen dan!" Ne migtabak ka me hibateen din ne migkahi, "Egmenuwen ney naa te eggimu sika te lalimma man de iya ne timman ka paan ta wey daruwa ne timman ne dereisek ne me isda."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-06.jpg}}

Ne impalalahan e ni Hisus te me hibateen din ka me etew te egpinnuu diye te due me hilamunen. Impunduk dan ka me etew te tiglalimma ne pulu te tagse punduk.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg}}

Nataman, ingkuwe e ni Hisus ka lalimma ne timman ne paan wey ka daruwa ne isda. Migleng-ag sikandin diye te langit wey impasalamatan din ka Manama para te keenen.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-08.jpg}}

Then Jesus broke the bread and the fish into pieces. He gave the pieces to his disciples to give to the people. The disciples kept passing out the food, and it never ran out! All the people ate and were satisfied.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-09.jpg}}

After that, the disciples collected the food that had not been eaten and it was enough to fill twelve baskets! All the food came from the five loaves of bread and two fish.
