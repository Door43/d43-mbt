====== 24.Inbawutismuwan ni Huwan si Hisus ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-01.jpg}}

Si Huwan ne anak ni Sakariyas wey Ilisabit,migdakel e wey neyimue ne prupita. Ne si kandin diye mig ugpe te disirtu,ne ka kineen din,teneb,wey talangas,wey ka kumbale din hinimu puun te bulbul te Kamiyu.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-02.jpg}}

Masalig ne me etew ne miggendiye te disirtu eyew te pegpammineg te wali rin,ne migkahi, "Inniyuhi niyu ka me sale niyu se mahaan e ka peghari te Manama! "



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-03.jpg}}

Wey masalig ka me etew ne migpammineg te wali ni Huwan mayitenged te pehinulsul te me sale,nataman inbawutismuwan din e si kandan.Ne dutu masalig ne me pangulu ne miggendiye eyew eg pabawutismu ki Huwan,piru ware si kandan mig inniyug te me sale dan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-04.jpg}}

Ne migkahi si Huwan diye te panglu te simbaan te me hudiyu.Si kaniyu se egpekeiling te uled!Balbalawi niyu ka pegke-etew niyu.Ka tagse kayu ne kene eg behey te meupiya ne behas eg tamperen seini wey igdebpak diye te eg kanlabkanlab ne hapey. "Si Huwan ka egkahiyen te me prupita ne miggenendue, "andama iyu ka dal



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-05.jpg}}

Ne due hudiyu ne mig-inse ki Huwan,ke sikandin naan-en iya ka Misiyas.Migtabak si Huwan, "Kene ne si keddiey ka Misiyas,piru due eggendini ne egpakasinundul keddi.Amana ne Maresen si kandin,ne minsan si keddi kenaan likes ne eggekad te hiket te sandal rin. "



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-06.jpg}}

Te seup ne aldew miggendiye si Hisus ki Huwan eyew eg pegpabawutismu.Te pegkakita ni Huwan kandin,migkahi rin te, "Kayid e ka Nati ne Karniru te Manama ne eg-awe te me sale te me etew kayi te ampew te tane."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-07.jpg}}

Ne inkahiyan ni Huwan si Hisus, "Kenaan likes ne egbawutis mu keykew.Si kediey ka keilangan neg bawutismuwan nu,"Piru migkahi si Hisus, "Bawutismuwi  a, keilangan ne eggimuwen ta seini eyew te pegtuman te igkeupii te Manama."Ne imbawutismuwan e ni Huwan si Hisus,minsan ware mule sale  Hisus.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-08.jpg}}

Te nabawutismuwan e wey miggakap e si Hisus puun te weyig,miglene diye te kandin ka panisingan te Manama ne egpekeiling te selepati.Ne due laheng te Manama ne egpuun diye te langit ne miggenen due, "Si keykew ka pinalangge kun Anak,ne amanaa nahale keykew."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-24-09.jpg}}

Ne miglalahan te Manama si Huwan te migkahi, "Ka Panisingan te Manama egleneb due te tagse sabeka kaniyu eyew te pegbawutismu.Ne ka seini ne etew Anak te Manama." ne si kandin nasabbeka re te Manama.Te pegbawutismu ni Huwan ki Hisus,nakarineg si kandin te lahung te Manama ne Amey ne miglalag,
