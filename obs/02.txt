====== F>Ka sale nakaseled te kalibutan ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-01.jpg}}

Amana nahale si Adan wey ka asawa rin ne mig-ugpe te amana ne meupiya ne pinamulaan ne imbehey te Manama kandan. Ne ware pad mule me kumbale dan, piru ware dan sika ikeyilew su ware mem pad sale kayi te kalibutan. Layun sikandan egleug-leug diye te pinamulaan, wey egpakiglalag te Manama.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-02.jpg}}

Piru due mule uled diye te pinamulaan ne amana ne utekan ne mig-inse te malitan ne miggenendue, "Kene kew bes egpakeenen te Manama te behas te langun ne kayu kayi te pinamulaan?"



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-03.jpg}}

Ne migtabak ka malitan, "Egkaayun key ne egkeen te behas te minsan nekey ne kayu, angin e te kayu ne egpakabehey te katuenan ke nekey ka meupiya wey mareet. Impanpanayan key te Manama ne egpatey key ke egkeen wey minsan ke eggen-gen key re due."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-04.jpg}}

Piru migkahi ka uled, "Kene ne malehet sika! Kene kew egpatey, piru nakataha ka Manama ne emun ke egkeen kew due, egpekeiling kew e kandin te nakataha ke nekey ka meupiya wey mareet."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-05.jpg}}

Ne nakita te malitan ne meupiya ka behas te kayu wey iling te meupiya seini ne egkeenen. Nataman, migpupu e sikandin te sabeka wey ingkeen din e seini su egkeupian degma sikandin ne egmatagseb. Ne imbehayan din e ka asawa rin, wey migkeen e degma sikandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-06.jpg}}

Ne due-rue ne nakasuman-suman sikandan, wey nakita ran e ne migpalawas bes mule sikandan. Ne imbunbunan dan en ubag ka me lawa ran te inteyi dan dagdahawi ne me derewunen eyew igbebaag dan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-07.jpg}}

Ne nerineg e enni Adan ka Manama ne eggipanew diye te pinamulaan, wey miggeles e sikandan. Ne migpangumew e ka Manama ne migkahi, "Hendei kew e?" Ne migtabak si Adan ka migkahi, "Narineg ku sikeykew ka eggipanew, purisu naaldek ad su ware ma kumbale ku, wey miggeles ad e."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-08.jpg}}

Ne mig-insaan e sikandan te Manama, "Hentew ka migkahi kaniyu ne migpalawas kew? Migkeen kew te behas te kayu ne kene ku igpakeen kaniyu?" Ne migtabak si Adan ka migkahi, "Seini la iya se malitan ne imbehey nu ka migpakeen keddi te behas te kayu." Ne migkahiyan e te Manama ka malitan, "Mania te inggimu nu seini?" Ne migkahi e ka malitan te in-akalan sikandin te uled.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-09.jpg}}

Ne migkahiyan e te Manama ka uled, "Indilus kad! Egdul-ug ka te tane wey egkeen ka te alinepung. Egpeg-ehetey kew te malitan minsan ka me anak niyu. Ne egdekdeken te kabuhalan te malitan ka ulu nu, wey egkahaten nu degma ka palu rin."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-10.jpg}}

God then said to the woman, “I will make childbirth very painful for you. You will desire your husband, and he will rule over you.”



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-11.jpg}}

God said to the man, “You listened to your wife and disobeyed me. Now the ground is cursed, and you will need to work hard to grow food. Then you will die, and your body will return to dirt.” The man named his wife Eve, which means “life-giver,” because she would become the mother of all people. And God clothed Adam and Eve with animal skins.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-02-12.jpg}}

Then God said, “Now that the human beings have become like us by knowing good and evil, they must not be allowed to eat the fruit of the tree of life and live forever.” So God sent Adam and Eve away from the beautiful garden. God placed powerful angels at the entrance to the garden to keep anyone from eating the fruit of the tree of life.
