====== F>Ka inggimu te Manama ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-01.jpg}}

Iling kayi ka puunan te langun ne neyitabu. Inggimu te Manama ka kalibutan wey ka langun kayi te hen-em ne aldew. Ne pegkeimpus te Manama te eggimu te kalibutan, marusilem seini wey ware pad minsan nekey ne tahu kayi. Piru ka Panisingan te Manama, diye te dibabew te weyig.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-02.jpg}}

Then God said, “Let there be light!” And there was light. God saw that the light was good and called it “day.” He separated it from the darkness, which he called “night.” God created the light on the first day of creation.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-03.jpg}}

Te igkarangeb ne aldew, miglalag ka Manama wey neyimu rin e ka aw-awangan/langit. Inggimu rin ka aw-awangan wey impegsuwey rin ka weyig diye te dibabew wey ka weyig kayi te diralem.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-04.jpg}}

Ne te igkatelu ne aldew, miglalag ka Manama wey impegsuwey rin e ka weyig wey tane, wey ingnaranan din ka mammara ne tane te "kalibutan", wey ingngaranan din ka weyig te "dahat." Ne nakita te Manama ne meupiya ka inggimu rin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-05.jpg}}

Then God said, “Let the earth produce all kinds of trees and plants.” And that is what happened. God saw that what he had created was good.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-06.jpg}}

Ne te igkeep-at ne aldew te peggimu, miglalag ka Manama wey neyimu e ka aldew, bulan, wey me bituen. Inggimu seini te Manama eyew egbehey te kalayag te kalibutan, wey palinneu te aldew wey marusilem, te timpu wey leg-un. Ne nakita te Manama ne meupiya ka inggimu rin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-07.jpg}}

Te igkalimma ne aldew, miglalag e man-e ka Manama, wey neyimu rin e ka langun ne diye te weyig wey ka langun ne me manuk-manuk. Ne nakita te Manama ne meupiya ka inggimu rin, wey impanalanginan din sikandan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-08.jpg}}

Te igkeen-em e ne aldew, migkahi e man-e ka Manama, "Egkeyimu ka langun ne mananap kayi te kalibutan!" Ne duen en iya ka langun iling te ingkahi rin. Due me paa te duma, egmandul-ug ka duma, wey me mahintalunan ka duma. Ne nakita te Manama ne meupiya ka inggimu rin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-09.jpg}}

Ne migkahi e ka Manama, "Eggimu ki te me etew ne iling kanta ne egbantey te kalibutan wey te langun ne me mananap."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-10.jpg}}

Ne sikan de iya ne migkuwe e ka Manama te tane ne impeiling din te etew. Imbehayan din seini te geyinawa wey migngaranan din e sikandin ki Adan. Miggimu pad man-e ka Manama te pinamulaan ne eg-ugpaan wey igpatanggu rin ki Adan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-11.jpg}}

Ne diye te taliware te pinamulaan, migpamula ka Manama te daruwa ne kayu ne inteil din. Ka kayu ne egpakabehey te umul ne ware egtamanan, wey ka kayu ne egpakabehey te katuenan ke nekey ka matareng wey mareet. Ne migkahiyan te Manama si Adan, "Egkaayun ka ne egkeen te minsan nekey ne behas te me kayu diye te pinamulaan, angin e te  kayu ne egpakiabehey te katuenan ke nekey ka meupiya wey mareet. Emun ke egkeen ka due ne egpatey ka iya."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-12.jpg}}

Ne migkahi ka Manama te kene ne meupiya ne egsabsabeka ka lukes. Piru ware minsan sabeka te me mananap ne egpakabulig kandin.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-13.jpg}}

Purisu impalipereng din e si Adan wey ingkuwa rin e ka sabeka ne gusuk din, wey inggimu rin e seini ne malitan. Ne in-uyan din e ka malitan diye ki Adan.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-14.jpg}}

When Adam saw her, he said, “At last! This one is like me! Let her be called ‘Woman,’ for she was made from Man.” This is why a man leaves his father and mother and becomes one with his wife.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-15.jpg}}

God made man and woman in his own image. He blessed them and told them, “Have many children and grandchildren and fill the earth!” And God saw that everything he had made was very good, and he was very pleased with all of it. This all happened on the sixth day of creation.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-01-16.jpg}}

When the seventh day came, God had finished his work. So God rested from all he had been doing. He blessed the seventh day and made it holy, because on this day he rested from his work. This is how God created the universe and everything in it.
